# Privacy Policy — Universe Chronicles

Last updated: June 2026

Universe Chronicles ("the App") is an interactive visual novel experience.

## Information We Collect
We do not collect personal information beyond what is necessary to operate the App, such as anonymous usage data (e.g. story choices made) used to show aggregate statistics within the App.

## How We Use Information
Any data collected is used solely to provide and improve the App experience, such as displaying community choice percentages.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

## Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through GitHub.
